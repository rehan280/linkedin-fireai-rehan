Contrarian that earns shares at 8:30am. FabAlley win at 1pm.

Vipul Personal Contrarian Hot Take
Tuesday · 8:30 AM IST
Your BI tool is not the problem. You are. I know that's harsh. But after 200+ conversations with Indian founders, the pattern is always the same. They blame the tool. The tool is fine. The problem is you're making 10-crore decisions from a spreadsheet nobody updated since Thursday. I watched a D2C founder burn 3 months of runway because their stock data was 4 days behind their sales data. Excel didn't fail them. Their process did. Here's what actually kills businesses: The gap between the question and the answer. "What's my net margin per SKU this week?" If that takes 4 hours, that is the problem. not the tool. Fix the process first. Then pick the tool. Connect every data source. Make the numbers live. Make answers available in 30 seconds. That's a process upgrade, not a technology upgrade. What is the most expensive question your team takes too long to answer?
