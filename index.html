Execution Notes

How to Get Maximum
Reach from This Series

Algorithm Rules
  • No external links in the post body. Always drop the fireai.in link in the first comment.
  • Post between 8-10 am or 12-1 pm IST Tuesday to Thursday for best reach.
  • Reply to every comment within the first 90 minutes. This keeps the post alive.
  • Max 3 hashtags per post. Place them at the bottom or in the first comment.
  • Tag a maximum of 2-3 relevant people per post. Tagging 10+ kills reach.
Media Execution Tips
  • Day 1 video: Keep under 60 seconds. Vertical format. First 3 seconds must have text on screen.
  • Day 2 graphic: Use FireAI's dark brand palette. Show 3 metrics in bold type. No clutter.
  • Day 3 carousel: 4-5 slides max. Each slide = one insight. Last slide always has a CTA.
  • Day 4 photo: Real photo of Vipul. Not a stock image. Authenticity beats polish on LinkedIn.
  • Day 5 poll: Let the post run as a LinkedIn native poll. Controversy drives comments and reach.
Engagement Strategy
  • Before each post goes live, spend 20 minutes commenting on 5-8 posts in your niche.
  • Vipul should comment from his personal profile on every FireAI post within the first hour.
  • Pin the best comment from Day 1 to build continuity across the series.
  • At the end of Day 5, post a recap on Vipul's personal profile linking the series.
Series Continuity
  • Use consistent visual branding across all 5 posts so followers recognise the series.
  • Start each post with a mini callback: "Earlier this week we talked about X..." on Days 3-5.
  • Track saves and comments per post. The top-performing format gets doubled down next month.
